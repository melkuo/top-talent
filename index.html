FAQ

Who is behind Top Talent?

Top Talent is run by the founders of Studio 535.

We're not professional recruiters. We're Product people who have worked for and hired at big tech companies and startups (ex-Shopify, ex-Uber). We deeply understand the problems that both great talent and great companies face, and we're building a better way to hire.

What roles are you hiring for?

We work with tech companies hiring for all roles – engineering, product design, product management, operations, growth, and sales.

Where does Top Talent operate?

We are focused on talent and companies located in the US and Canada. Our network is strongest in Toronto, San Francisco and New York City.

Do you have remote roles?

Yes, we work with companies hiring both onsite and remote. You'll be able to specify your preference on sign-up.

How do I refer friends?

When your friend signs-up for Top Talent, they'll be asked, "How did you hear about Top Talent?". They can share your name then.

If your friend gets hired through Top Talent, we'll send you $250. No account required.
